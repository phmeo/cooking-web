Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Oil for pan

Equipment

  • Mixing bowls and whisk
  • Nonstick skillet
  • Spatula

Nutrition (approx. per serving)

Calories380
Carbs52 g
Protein9 g
Fat14 g

Notes & Variations

  • Add blueberries or chocolate chips to batter.
  • Serve with butter, maple syrup, and fruit.
