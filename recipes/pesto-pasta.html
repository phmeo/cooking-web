Pesto Pasta

15 mins • Serves 2 • Easy
Ingredients Steps Equipment Nutrition Notes

Categories

🍝Italian 🥬Vegetarian ⚡Quick & Easy
⏱️
Prep5 mins
🍳
Cook10 mins
📦
Serves2
🎯
DifficultyEasy

Steps

Ingredients

  • 200 g pasta (fusilli or spaghetti)
  • 1/3 cup basil pesto
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper

Equipment

  • Large pot
  • Mixing bowl or skillet

Nutrition (approx. per serving)

Calories520
Carbs68 g
Protein14 g
Fat18 g

Notes & Variations

  • Add halved cherry tomatoes for freshness.
  • Swap basil pesto for arugula or spinach pesto.
