Chocolate Chip Cookies

35 mins • Makes 18 • Beginner
Ingredients Steps Equipment Nutrition Notes

Categories

🍰Desserts ⚡Quick & Easy
⏱️
Prep15 mins
🔥
Bake10–12 mins
🍪
Yield~18 cookies
🎯
DifficultyBeginner

Steps

Tip: For thicker cookies, chill scooped dough 30 minutes before baking.

Ingredients

  • 115 g unsalted butter, softened
  • 100 g brown sugar
  • 70 g white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 160 g all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 170 g chocolate chips or chunks

Equipment

  • Stand mixer or hand mixer
  • Mixing bowls and spatula
  • Baking sheets and parchment

Nutrition (approx. per cookie)

Calories150
Carbs18 g
Protein2 g
Fat8 g
Sodium60 mg

Notes & Variations

  • Swap half the chocolate for chopped nuts.
  • Add a pinch of cinnamon or espresso powder for depth.
  • Storage: airtight 3–4 days; freeze dough balls up to 2 months.
