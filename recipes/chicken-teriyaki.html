Steps

Tip: Starting in a cold pan maximizes fat rendering and skin crispness without burning.

Ingredients

  • 600 g chicken thighs, skin-on, boneless
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 2 tbsp sake (or water + 1 tsp sugar)
  • 1 tbsp honey or sugar
  • Sesame seeds and scallions to finish

Equipment

  • Nonstick skillet
  • Tongs and knife
  • Instant-read thermometer (optional)

Nutrition (approx. per serving)

Calories420
Carbs10 g
Protein28 g
Fat28 g
Sodium980 mg

Notes & Variations

  • Swap thighs for chicken breast (reduce cook time).
  • Add 1 tsp ginger and 1 clove garlic to sauce for extra depth.
  • For a thicker glaze, stir in 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 1 tbsp water.
