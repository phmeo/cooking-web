Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

15 mins • Serves 2 • Easy
Ingredients Steps Equipment Nutrition Notes

Categories

🍝Italian 🥬Vegetarian ⚡Quick & Easy
⏱️
Prep5 mins
🍳
Cook10 mins
📦
Serves2
🎯
DifficultyEasy

Steps

Tip: Emulsification is key—toss pasta with oil and starchy water off the heat to create a silky sauce.

Ingredients

  • 200 g spaghetti
  • 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3–4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Optional: lemon wedge, parmesan

Equipment

  • Large pot (at least 4 L)
  • Wide skillet or sauté pan
  • Tongs and ladle

Nutrition (approx. per serving)

Calories520
Carbs68 g
Protein12 g
Fat20 g
Sodium560 mg

Notes & Variations

  • Add anchovies with the garlic for deeper umami.
  • Stir in 1 tbsp butter at the end for extra gloss.
  • Swap parsley for basil, or add toasted breadcrumbs for crunch.
