Garlic Butter Shrimp

12 mins • Serves 2 • Easy
Ingredients Steps Equipment Nutrition Notes

Categories

⚡Quick & Easy
⏱️
Prep5 mins
🍳
Cook7 mins
📦
Serves2
🎯
DifficultyEasy

Steps

Tip: Avoid overcooking—shrimp turn rubbery fast. Pull as soon as they curl and turn opaque.

Ingredients

  • 300 g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and pepper

Equipment

  • 12-inch skillet
  • Tongs

Nutrition (approx. per serving)

Calories260
Carbs3 g
Protein24 g
Fat16 g
Sodium780 mg

Notes & Variations

  • Add chili flakes for heat.
  • Swap parsley for basil or dill.
  • Toss with pasta and a splash of pasta water for a quick dinner.
